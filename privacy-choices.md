# StudyVIBE Privacy Choices

Last updated: 2026-04-08

This page explains privacy choices available to StudyVIBE users.

## Tracking and Advertising

StudyVIBE does not sell personal data and does not use cross-app tracking for third-party advertising.

## Access, Correction, and Deletion Requests

You may request:

- access to your personal data,
- correction of inaccurate data,
- deletion of your account data.
