# StudyVIBE Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-08

StudyVIBE ("we", "our", or "us") provides an AI-powered learning assistant application. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## 1. Data We Collect

Depending on how you use StudyVIBE, we may collect:

- Contact information (such as email address and, when provided by Apple Sign In, name)
- Account identifiers (such as your user ID)
- User content (such as notes, lesson text, assistant chat messages, and uploaded/recorded audio)
- Purchase/subscription information (such as subscription status and purchase history metadata)
- Usage data (such as in-app interaction and feature usage metrics)

We do not collect data for third-party advertising tracking.

## 2. How We Use Data

We use collected data to:

- Provide core app functionality
- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Process subscriptions and enforce plan limits
- Generate AI-powered outputs (for example summaries, quizzes, and assistant responses)
- Improve app quality, reliability, and performance

## 3. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted service providers to operate StudyVIBE, such as:

- Supabase (authentication, database, storage, backend services)
- Apple (in-app purchases, subscription events, platform services)
- AI/TTS providers used by app features

These providers process data only as needed to deliver their services to StudyVIBE.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your data as described below.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your region, you may have rights to:

- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to or restrict certain processing

To make a privacy request, contact us at: `support@studyvibe.app`

## 6. Children's Privacy

StudyVIBE is not directed to children under 13 (or a higher age where required by local law). If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made.

## 9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

`support@studyvibe.app`
